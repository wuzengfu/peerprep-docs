---
title: "Database Choices"
description: "Understanding the database choices for each microservice."
---

## Question Service

- Choice: **MongoDB**
- Reasons:
  - Complex & nested schema.
  - High availability & scalability.
  - Unlikely to be changed once created.

## History Service

- Choice: **MongoDB**
- Reasons:
  - Complex & nested schema.
  - High availability & scalability.
  - Unlikely to be changed once created.

## User Service

- Choice: **PostgreSQL**
- Reasons:
  - Data integrity & consistency as user information is critical.
  - Strict schema to prevent users from having bad/inconsistent data.
